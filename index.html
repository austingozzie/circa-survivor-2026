⚡ Core Principles
  • Future Value is everything. Save KC, DET, BUF, PHI for Wk 12+. Never burn an S-tier team on a C+ spot.
  • Plan all 20 weeks before Week 1. You'll need 4–5 elite teams, 9–10 playoff-caliber teams, and 6+ "gross" moderate favorites.
  • Contrarian only when it counts. Fade the crowd when the chalk has real upset risk AND crowd concentration is high. Never go contrarian just to be different.
  • Scoop equity late. In the final 4 weeks, prioritize picks that differ from other survivors — even at slight EV cost — to maximize solo-win odds.
  • EV over raw win odds. The 2024 winners consistently took higher-EV teams, not just the biggest favorites.

🎰 Large Pool Game Theory

Circa had 14,000+ entries in 2024. This changes everything vs. a small office pool:

  • Playing it safe every week guarantees you share the pot with hundreds of others if you survive.
  • If 60% of the pool picks the same team and they lose, your equity skyrockets — but only if your pick wins.
  • In 2023, only 4% of entries made it to Thanksgiving. Plan like it'll go the full 20 weeks.
  • Consider portfolio diversification across your 10 entries — don't put all entries on the same team in a given week.
  • Entries can be sold mid-season — factor in liquidity milestones if you're managing multiple.
✅ Don't Miss Your Picks

In 2023, 31 entrants burned $31,000 by simply forgetting to submit. Their dead money is your edge.

  • Set a recurring Saturday alarm at 3PM PT for regular weeks.
  • Set a Wednesday alarm the week of Thanksgiving.
  • Set a Thursday 8AM alarm on December 25.
  • If using a proxy service, confirm they received your pick every single week.
  • Non-Nevada residents must register in person and use a proxy for weekly submissions.

🏈 How Picks Work
Format
Pick one NFL team to win straight-up each week. No spreads. No point totals. Just pick a winner.
Team Reuse
Each team can only be used once per entry across the entire contest. No restriction on picking against the same team multiple times.
Total Picks
Up to 20 picks in 2026: NFL Weeks 1–18 plus Thanksgiving leg and Christmas leg.
Elimination
If your selected team loses or ties, your entry is eliminated immediately.
Ties
A tie result eliminates your entry — treated the same as a loss.
